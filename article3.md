GroqAI assistant in the app expense tracker is built to understand your data and give you accurate insights, tips and results based on your data. It runs on the Llama Model family,
exactly the model "Llama 3.4.1 versatile".
* There can be two errors in the AI while usage. The solution to both is to wait for a while and then try again. It could be an AI connection error or you might have finished your
tokens. We are working on adding a payment page so you can directly access Groq and pay for higher token limits.
* How to Use the AI
