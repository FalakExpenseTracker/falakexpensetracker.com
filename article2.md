Troubleshooting & Common Issues
1. App Crashes on Startup (Installed via APK)
•
Cause: Encrypted database libraries (SQLCipher) fail to load or the device's architecture is not supported.
•
Solution: Ensure you are installing the latest version of the APK. If the problem persists, go to Settings > Apps > Expense Tracker > Clear Cache and try again.
2. Transactions Not Syncing Between Devices
•
Cause: You might be using different Google accounts, or the background sync is restricted by your device's "Battery Optimization" settings.
•
Solution:

3. "Biometric Authentication Required" Loop
•
Cause: The device's biometric system (fingerprint/face) was changed, or the secure hardware is temporarily unavailable.
•
Solution: Restart your phone. If it still fails, you can reset the security lock by logging out and logging back in (Note: Offline-only data may be lost if not synced to the cloud).
4. Missing Categories or Currency Symbols
•
Cause: A currency conversion was interrupted or the local preferences were cleared.
•
Solution: Go to the Main Menu > Currency and re-select your preferred currency. This will trigger a recalculation of all entries.
5. Voice Input Not Working
•
Cause: Google Speech Services are not installed or "Microphone" permission was denied.
•
Solution: Check your device permissions in Settings > Apps > Expense Tracker > Permissions and ensure Microphone is allowed. Also, ensure the Google App is up to date.
6. Chart Data Looks Incorrect
•
Cause: The "Analysis Dashboard" might be filtered to a specific month where no data exists.
•
Solution: Use the Date Range Selector at the top of the Analysis page to select "All Time" or the specific year you want to view.
