This app Expense Tracker is still under development. All crashes or bugs, kindly report at the app store.
The Expense Tracker team shall work to give you a new version with bug fixes. For the most common errors, they are:
+ No Data on New version installation:
+ This is because your Room database didn't find any migration code. If you have a backup, you are saved. If you dont,well, say bye to your data.
+ AI Not returning anything:
+ This is a pure bug. Please give this feedback on the app store. Download a new version of the app maximum 5 days later.
+ Your Data taking some time to load:
+ If you have thousands of transactions, this is normal. Please wait for a minute or so.
